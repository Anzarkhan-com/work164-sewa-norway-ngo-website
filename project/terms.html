Food quality must be maintained and will be checked.
The food stall owner shall be responsible for damages (if any) due to the serving of bad quality food.
Organizers may claim the reputation damages for the event and organization due to improper quality of food.

Food stall owners will be responsible for cleanliness in and around their stall and especially at the bhojan area. Event organisers reserved the rights to ask for refundable deposit upto Kr. 500 to ensure proper cleaning is done by the stall owners.
